Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is a family favorite. It is a layered pasta dish with meat, cheese, and tomato sauce. This recipe is a classic lasagna recipe that is perfect for feeding a crowd. It is easy to make and is sure to be a hit with your family and friends.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices & Seasoning

Instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest
